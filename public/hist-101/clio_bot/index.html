Getting started · Upload your Historian's Table notes
Begin with your own work. Upload the .txt file created by the worksheet's Export for Clio button. Clio will read your evidence notes, first interpretation, and historian responses before joining the conversation.
Clio may notice obvious repeated answers or a sharp change in writing style. A style reminder is not an AI detector, does not determine authorship, and does not block progress. Its purpose is to keep the historical thinking yours.
Five short rounds. Clio challenges your interpretation but does not write your final judgment. Transcript buttons unlock only after Clio's closing message.
