Study Buddy The Foundation of Ethical Reasoning

Before we can think clearly about ethics, we need to understand how we think. This lecture explores worldviews as the "operating system" of the mind, the psychology of moral intuition (Haidt's elephant and rider), and the critical thinking skills needed to examine our own assumptions.
