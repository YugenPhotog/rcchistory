Review Questions Covered

Q3 — Kangxi’s Consolidation of the Qing Empire: Evaluate the strategies the Kangxi emperor utilized to solidify Manchu rule over China. How did he neutralize internal rebellions and secure the empire's vast northern and western frontiers?
Q5 — The Dual Rulership and Cultural Control of Qianlong: Analyze the Qianlong emperor's approach to ruling a massive, multiethnic empire. How did he balance his patronage of diverse cultural traditions with his strict suppression of anti-Manchu sentiment?
Q6 — The Canton System and the Macartney Mission: Examine the Qing Dynasty's regulation of European trade during the eighteenth century. What were the objectives of the British Macartney Mission of 1793, and why did the Qianlong emperor ultimately reject them?
Q7 — Demographic Boom and the Agricultural Economy: Identify the primary factors that contributed to the doubling of the Chinese population during the eighteenth century. What were the social and economic consequences of this massive demographic shift?
