How to Complete This Assignment

This worksheet asks you to trace the transformation of labor in colonial America — from the ambiguous, negotiable status of the earliest African workers to the fully codified system of chattel slavery. You are building a timeline in five phases.

Each phase has two required sources. Use the documentary for the human stories — real people, real moments, real voices. Use your Foner readings for the legal and structural framework — the laws, codes, and economic systems that turned individual moments into permanent institutions.

Click each phase header to open it. Fill in what you know. When all five phases are complete, click Export at the bottom — then paste your timeline into the Professor Harriet chatbot for feedback before submitting.
