Give Me Liberty!

Guided Reading Guides  ·  8-Week Accelerated  ·  Select a chapter to open its guide

How to use these guides: Each guide opens in a new browser tab and is built to be completed while you read the assigned textbook chapter. The questions, key terms, and forensic anchor sentences follow the structure of Give Me Liberty! directly — open the chapter alongside your guide, print the guide, and fill it in by hand as you read.

⚠️ Quiz Connection: The guided reading quizzes on Canvas draw directly from the anchor sentences and review questions in these guides. A completed, handwritten guide is your best quiz prep tool. Students who skip the guide and rely on skimming consistently underperform on the Anchor Sentence questions — those require word-for-word recognition.
